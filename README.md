🍔 Food Delivery Sales & Order Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Food Delivery Sales & Order Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to monitor restaurant performance, customer ordering behavior, delivery efficiency, and payment trends. The dashboard transforms raw food delivery transaction data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filtering.

This dashboard helps restaurant managers, food delivery businesses, and business analysts identify sales trends, optimize delivery operations, improve customer satisfaction, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze food delivery operations and answer key business questions such as:

Which restaurant receives the highest number of orders?
What is the overall delivery success rate?
Which payment method is preferred by customers?
How does food demand change over time?
What are the cancellation trends?
Which food categories generate the highest order volume?

The dashboard provides a centralized view of operational and sales performance.

Business Problem

Food delivery businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to:

Track delivery performance
Monitor restaurant-wise sales
Analyze payment preferences
Identify cancellation patterns
Understand customer ordering trends

Manual reporting often leads to delayed decision-making.

The dashboard aims to:

✔ Track delivery efficiency

✔ Analyze customer ordering behavior

✔ Compare restaurant performance

✔ Identify popular food categories

✔ Understand payment method trends

✔ Support operational and strategic decision-making
